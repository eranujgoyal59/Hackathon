Welcome to AI

We can predict dry wet status with artificial intelligence

Training

AI is ready for training. Train AI with two different set of samples according to the given instructions. These samples help AI to learn about two different types of items. Then AI will automatically recognize the items based on given input.

Prediction

AI is ready for prediction. Show the different items to it and it will identify which item it is what.

Instructions

Follow the below instructions for training and prediction of AI -

  1. First enable webcam and allow access when asked.
  2. Now find an object, point cam at it, click and hold gather class 1 data button to gather at least 30 samples.
  3. Repeat for class 2 with a different object of interest. Get similar number of samples.
  4. Click train and predict and wait while the model is trained live in your browser. No data is sent to server.
  5. Once trained you will see live predictions appear above the video for what it thinks it sees.
